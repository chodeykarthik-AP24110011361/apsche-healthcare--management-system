# apsche-healthcare--management-system
AI-powered Smart Healthcare Management System with Twilio SMS, Google Sheets, Google Calendar and AI Health Summary.

our website url:- https://apsche-healthcare-management-system.vercel.app/



## Introduction:-

Welcome to the demonstration of RK Health AI, an AI-powered Smart Healthcare Management System.

Our application is designed to simplify healthcare management by providing a centralized platform for managing patient records, appointments, medications, AI-generated health summaries, SMS notifications, and Google Calendar integration.

The project is developed using React.js, Vite, Google Apps Script, Google Sheets, Groq AI, Twilio SMS, Google Calendar, GitHub, and Vercel.



## Dashboard:-

This is the main dashboard of our application.

The dashboard provides a quick overview of the healthcare system by displaying:

-Total Patients

-Today's Appointments

-Active Prescriptions

-Pending Reminders


This helps administrators monitor the overall status of the healthcare system from a single screen.






## Patient Management:-

Next, we have the Patient Management module.

Here, administrators can:

-Add new patients

-View patient details

-Edit patient information

-Delete patient records


Each patient profile securely stores personal and medical information.

One of the key features of our project is the Generate AI Summary option.

"Using Groq AI, the system generates a concise health summary based on the patient's medical information, allowing healthcare professionals to quickly understand the patient's condition".






## Appointment Management:-

This module allows administrators to:

-Book appointments

-View scheduled appointments

-Update appointment details

-Cancel appointments


While booking an appointment, we enter the patient's details, doctor's name, department, appointment date, time, and phone number.

Once the appointment is confirmed:

The appointment details are stored securely in Google Sheets.
A Google Calendar event is created automatically.
A confirmation SMS notification is sent to the patient's registered mobile number using Twilio.

This automation reduces manual effort and ensures that patients receive timely reminders.







## Medication Management:-

The Medication Management module helps administrators manage patient prescriptions efficiently.

Using this module, we can:

-Add medications

-Update prescriptions

-Track medication schedules

-Monitor medicine availability


This helps maintain accurate medication records and improves patient care.








## Reports:-

The Reports module provides an organized view of healthcare data.

Administrators can generate reports related to patients, appointments, and healthcare activities, enabling better monitoring and decision-making.







## Backend:-

Our backend is developed using Google Apps Script.

All patient records, appointments, and medication details are securely stored in Google Sheets, which acts as the cloud database.

The backend also integrates securely with:

-Twilio SMS

-Google Calendar

-Groq AI


through API-based communication.
